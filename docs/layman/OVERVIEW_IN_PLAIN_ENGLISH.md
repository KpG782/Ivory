# Ivory In Plain English

Ivory is a dental front-desk assistant that can do two jobs in one chat:

1. answer dental and clinic questions
2. help a patient set up a visit step by step

## Why this project exists

Most chatbots are good at talking but bad at completing structured workflows.

Most forms are good at collecting data but bad at handling natural conversation.

This project tries to combine both.

## What makes it useful

The patient can:

- ask what services the clinic offers
- ask what to do about a toothache
- start booking a cleaning, an emergency visit, or a cosmetic consultation
- answer one question at a time
- interrupt the booking to ask another question
- continue the booking from the same place
- accept the visit and have the front-desk chores (CRM lead, booking request, confirmation email) handled automatically

## What makes it safer than a pure AI chatbot

The cost estimate is not generated by the AI model.

Instead:

- the AI helps answer dental knowledge questions
- the backend handles workflow steps and the visit estimate with fixed rules

Every estimate is clearly labeled as educational — not a diagnosis and not a final price. That means the important business logic is more controlled and easier to explain.
