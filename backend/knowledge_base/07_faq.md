# Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions the front desk hears most often at Ivory Dental Studio. If your question is not covered here, call (415) 555-0100 during business hours or ask Ivory, the AI front desk assistant, any time.

## I'm a new patient. What happens at my first visit?

Your first visit is a comprehensive exam: a health history review, a full check of your teeth and gums, digital X-rays if needed, an oral cancer screening, and — when time and gum health allow — a cleaning at the same appointment. Plan for about 60 to 90 minutes. You will leave with a clear picture of your oral health and, if any treatment is recommended, a written cost estimate before anything is scheduled.

## What should I bring?

- A photo ID.
- Your dental insurance card, if you have one.
- A list of medications you take, including over-the-counter supplements.
- Any recent dental X-rays from a previous office, if available (your old office can email them to hello@ivorydentalstudio.example).

## Do I need to fill out forms in advance?

After you book, we email a link to our secure online intake forms — they take about ten minutes. If you prefer paper, arrive 15 minutes early and we will provide them at the front desk.

## What are your hours? Do you offer evenings or weekends?

We are open Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and closed Sundays and major holidays. Late-afternoon slots (after 4:00 PM) and Saturday appointments are our busiest — book those one to two weeks ahead. When Ivory asks for your preferred time of day, "evening" means the last appointments of the day, starting around 4:00 PM.

## What is your cancellation policy?

Life happens — we just ask for **24 hours' notice** so another patient can use the slot. You can cancel or reschedule by phone at (415) 555-0100. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice, or missed without notice, may incur a $50 late-cancellation fee. Emergencies are always the exception; just tell us what happened.

## Where do I park?

Free patient parking is in the lot behind the Pearl Street Commons building, with two accessible spaces next to the elevator entrance. Street parking on Pearl Street is metered until 6:00 PM. We are on the second floor, Suite 210, reachable by stairs or elevator.

## Do you see children?

Yes — we see the whole family, and children are welcome from their first tooth onward. See our children and family dentistry page for details on first visits, sealants, and fluoride.

## I'm anxious about dental visits. What do you offer?

Tell us — you will not be the only one that day. We offer noise-reducing headphones, blankets, extra explanation (or extra quiet, your choice), and an agreed hand-raise signal that pauses any procedure immediately. For longer procedures, ask about comfort options at your consultation.

## Do you handle dental emergencies?

Yes. We hold same-day slots for urgent problems Monday through Saturday, and an after-hours emergency line is available at (415) 555-0114. Ivory can start an emergency visit request at any hour by collecting your name, phone number, the type of problem, and your pain level.

## How does booking through Ivory actually work?

Ivory collects a short list of details that depends on the visit type, then produces an educational cost estimate. Nothing is committed until you say **accept** — you can also say **adjust** to change a detail or **restart** to begin over. Once you accept, the request goes to our front desk team, who confirm the exact appointment time with you directly. You can ask Ivory general questions mid-booking; your answers so far are never lost.

## Is what Ivory tells me medical advice?

No. Ivory shares educational information from this knowledge base and deterministic cost estimates. It does not diagnose, and its estimates are not final prices — the dentist's exam and the front desk's written quote are always the source of truth.
