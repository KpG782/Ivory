# Ivory Dental Studio — Clinic Overview

Ivory Dental Studio is a modern general and cosmetic dental practice built around calm, transparent, judgment-free care. Ivory, the AI front desk assistant, answers questions from this knowledge base and helps patients set up cleaning visits, emergency visits, and cosmetic consultations.

## What dental services do we offer

Ivory Dental Studio offers three main categories of care:

- **Routine exams and cleanings** — comprehensive checkups, professional cleanings, digital X-rays, fluoride treatment, and oral cancer screening.
- **Emergency and urgent visits** — same-day care for toothaches, chipped or broken teeth, swelling, lost fillings or crowns, and knocked-out teeth.
- **Cosmetic dentistry** — professional teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, clear aligners, and composite bonding, starting with a no-pressure cosmetic consultation.

We also provide family and pediatric checkups, dental sealants for children, and gum disease (periodontal) treatment, including deep cleanings.

## Our philosophy

We believe most dental anxiety comes from surprise — surprise pain, surprise costs, surprise procedures. Our approach is to remove surprises:

- Every visit starts with a clear explanation of what will happen and why.
- Cost estimates are shared before treatment, never after.
- Preventive care comes first; we recommend the least invasive option that solves the problem.
- No lectures. Whether your last visit was six months ago or ten years ago, you will be met with a plan, not a guilt trip.

## How booking works with Ivory

Ivory collects a few details depending on the type of visit:

- **Cleaning:** patient name, contact email, year of your last dental visit, insurance status (insured or self-pay), and a preferred time of day (morning, afternoon, or evening).
- **Emergency:** patient name, contact phone number, the type of problem (toothache, chipped tooth, swelling, or lost filling), and your current pain level from 0 to 10.
- **Cosmetic consultation:** patient name, contact email, the treatment you are interested in (whitening, veneers, aligners, or bonding), a budget band, and a timeline.

Ivory then produces an educational visit estimate — a cost range, not a diagnosis or a final price — and, once you accept, records the appointment request for the front desk team, who confirm the exact time slot.

## Accessibility and comfort

The studio is wheelchair accessible via the Pearl Street Commons elevator. We offer noise-reducing headphones, blankets, and clearly signaled hand-raise breaks during any procedure. Interpretation support for Spanish and Tagalog is available with advance notice.
