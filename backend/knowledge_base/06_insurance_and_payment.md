# Insurance and Payment Options

Ivory Dental Studio works with most PPO dental insurance plans and offers clear self-pay pricing for patients without coverage. Our patient coordinator, June Park, handles all insurance verification and payment questions — reach her at (415) 555-0100 or hello@ivorydentalstudio.example. This page explains what we accept, how billing works, and what to expect if you are paying out of pocket.

## Dental insurance plans we accept

We are in-network with most major PPO dental plans, including (fictional examples for this demo clinic):

- BlueOak Dental PPO

We also accept most out-of-network PPO plans — you can still be seen here, and we will submit claims on your behalf; your share of the cost may simply be higher than with an in-network plan. We do **not** currently participate in DHMO/capitation plans or state Medicaid dental programs, but we can suggest nearby clinics that do.

## How insurance affects your visit estimate

When you set up a visit through Ivory, the assistant asks whether you are **insured** or **self-pay**, because it changes the estimated patient responsibility:

- **Cleaning visits:** insured patients see their estimate multiplied by 0.45, reflecting the strong preventive-care coverage most PPO plans provide (many cover routine exams and cleanings at or near 100%, so 0.45 is a conservative planning figure).
- **Emergency visits:** insured patients see their estimate multiplied by 0.5, reflecting typical coverage for exams and X-rays.
- **Cosmetic treatments:** dental insurance does not cover elective cosmetic work — whitening, veneers, aligners, and bonding are always self-pay, and the estimate never applies an insurance discount.

These multipliers are planning tools, not promises. Your actual coverage depends on your specific plan's deductible, annual maximum, and waiting periods. Before your appointment, June verifies your benefits directly with your insurer and gives you a written pre-treatment estimate for anything beyond the visit itself.

## What to bring if you are insured

- Your dental insurance card (or the plan name, member ID, and group number).
- A photo ID.
- If your plan changed recently, bring the new card — claims sent to an old plan are the most common cause of billing delays.

We submit claims electronically, usually the same day as your visit. You pay your estimated share at checkout; if the insurer's final payment differs, we refund or bill the difference and explain why.

## Self-pay patients

No insurance is required to be a patient here. Self-pay patients receive the same deterministic estimate math published in our pricing guide, and payment is due at the time of the visit. For larger treatment plans, we provide a written, itemized quote before any work begins — never after.

## Ivory Smile Membership (for uninsured patients)

Our in-house membership plan (fictional demo detail) costs $29 per month and includes two routine exam-and-cleaning visits per year, routine X-rays, and 15% off other treatment performed at the studio. It is not insurance — there are no claims, deductibles, or waiting periods — but it makes preventive care predictable for households without dental benefits. Ask June to enroll.

## Payment methods and financing

- **Accepted payments:** cash, all major credit and debit cards, HSA and FSA cards, and contactless payment.
- **Payment plans:** treatment over $500 can be split into up to six monthly payments, interest-free, arranged before treatment starts.
- **Third-party financing:** longer financing terms are available through an outside healthcare lender for larger cosmetic or restorative plans; June can walk you through an application in about ten minutes.

## Estimates, always before treatment

Our philosophy is that costs should never be a surprise. Every estimate from Ivory — and every quote from our clinical team — is educational and provided before treatment, not after. If the dentist finds something during an exam that changes the plan, we stop, explain, and re-quote in writing before continuing.
